Services we offer

We provide a wide array of translation services that will benefit you and set your event or work apart in terms of audience reach.

Translation

We are experts and trusted partners in both verbal and written translations for documents, websites, movie scripts, research papers and whatever else you want translated. All this is done by humans so your work won't lose that human feel.

Transcription

We offer fast, accurate and confidential transcription for audio, video, plain text and subtitles, as well as add-ons including faster turnaround time, multilingual audio, timestamps and additional file types to add a proffessional flair to your work.

Proof Reading

Whether a simple typo or a more complex grammatical error, our qualified proofreaders check and double check your text to ensure itâ€™s perfect. We fix errors based on language structure, punctuation, grammar, consistency, format, structure and style.

Copywritting

Our expert copywriters will work their magic on almost any type of content including blog posts, website content, press releases, emails, social media content, white papers and taglines, to help you convert traffic into leads and finally, customers.

Quality Assesment

We will assess your text or audio files to determine whether they reach your agreed quality standards, including checking your speech to decide whether it sounds natural, or selection of the most relevant comments in a text.

Event Hosting(MC)

We realise sometimes you have a unique crowd that needs an extra bit of attention to reach, we have multilanguage experts who are charismatic, experienced public speakers who will make sure everyone attending gets involved.
